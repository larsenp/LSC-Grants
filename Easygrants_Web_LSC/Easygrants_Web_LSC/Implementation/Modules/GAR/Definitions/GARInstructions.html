Submission Requirements.

All programs receiving 2008 and 2009 LSC funding must submit:

(1) Actual Grant Activity Reports

(2) Other Services Reports and CSR Self-Inspection Reports

(3) Projected Grant Activity Reports

(4) Office and Staffing Information

Programs receiving funding for more than one LSC service area must complete some separate forms for each service area. All D Forms and the G-1, G-3, G-4 and G-5 Forms must be submitted separately for each service area, G-1(d), G-3(d), J-1, and J-2 are submitted only for Basic Field Service areas and, with one exception, no grantee has more than one Basic Field service area. All E Forms (Staffing), the G-6 Form (Self-Inspection) and Forms M and L are submitted only once for each grantee.
