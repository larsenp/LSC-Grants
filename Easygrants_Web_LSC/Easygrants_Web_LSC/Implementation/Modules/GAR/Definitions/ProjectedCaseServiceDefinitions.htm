Interim Case Service Definitions

Client Services include all forms of advice, brief service and extended service to low-income clients in cases involving all types of legal problems.

Limited Service includes counsel & advice and limited action.

Extended Service includes negotiation toward settlement (with or without litigation), administrative agency proceedings, litigation in court and other Extensive Services beyond advice and limited service, including transactional services which do not involve a controversy, and thus are not "settled" or litigated. These Extensive services include such assistance to clients as drafting complex legal documents, such as, contracts, wills, Advance Directives (simple legal documents fall under Limited Service), obtaining licenses or permits, purchasing or selling real estate and obtaining small business loans.

Type of Legal Problem: The list of types of legal problems, by definition, encompasses all types of cases that will be handled with Corporation funds.
