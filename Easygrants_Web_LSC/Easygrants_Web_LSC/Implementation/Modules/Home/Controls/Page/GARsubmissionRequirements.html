Submission Requirements

All D Forms and the G-1, G-3, G-4 and G-5 Forms must be submitted separately for each service area. G-1(d), G-3(d), J-1, and J-2 are submitted only for Basic Field Service areas and, with one exception, no grantee has more than one Basic Field service area. All E Forms (Staffing), the G-6 Form (Self-Inspection) and Forms M and L are submitted only once for each grantee.

Due Dates. The 2008-2009 Grant Activity Reports are due according to the following schedule:

March 2

Case Service Information (Actual and Projected) and CSR Self Inspection
 (Forms G-1, G-1(d), G-3, G-3(d), G-4, G-5, G-5(d), G-6)

March 16

Staffing, Budget and PAI Information (Actual and Projected)
 (Forms E-0,  E-1, E-2, D-1, D-2, D-3, D-4, J-1, and J-2)

Other Services Reports
(Forms M-1(a), M-1(b), M-1(c), M-2(a), M-2(b), M-3(a), M-3(b), M-4, M-5, M-6(a), M-6(b) and Form L, Narrative

All programs receiving both 2008 and 2009 LSC funding must submit:

  1. Actual Grant Activity Reports
  2. Other Services Reports and CSR Self-Inspection Reports
  3. Projected Grant Activity Reports (Not required for grantees receiving only 2008 funding)
  4. Office and Staffing Information
