LSC Year 2008 Grants Competition

Instructions for Application Checklist -- Form B

This section contains instructions for the Grant Application Checklist -- Form B.

 

The LSC Grant Application Forms are accessible for all applicants who have submitted the Notice of Intent to Compete (Form H).  To access the grant application forms, click Grant Applications from the Legal Services Corporation Applicant Information Network homepage (www.ain.lsc.gov). This will take you to the Competitive Grant Applications menu. Then select Grant Application Forms and enter the LSC Applicant ID and the Password you used for submitting the Notice of Intent to Compete form.

After you have entered your Applicant ID and password, you will access the Grant Application Forms Menu. Select Checklist - Form B and click 'OK' to continue.

Form B

Form B is the Grant Application Checklist.  It is completed after all of the other forms and materials in the grant application package are completed. 

There are three sections to Form B: 

In section (a) of Form B, enter the total number of pages included in each document transmitted to LSC as a PDF file.

In section (b) of Form B, enter the total number of pages included in the proposal narrative, the resumes of key staff, references, disciplinary complaints, subgrant information, and the governing body plan.

Section (c) of Form B, is completed automatically as the applicant completes and submits forms A, D, F, K, L, and any applicable supplemental forms.  Applicants must check this section to confirm that all required forms have been submitted to LSC. The number of pages for the electronic forms will appear automatically as you submit each form. If all required documents have been submitted, 'Yes' will appear in the right column. If 'No' appears, one or more required forms have not been submitted.  Because Form B tracks submission of all forms, please submit this form after you have submitted all the other required forms.

To save, exit or submit, use the buttons at the bottom of the screen.

Please note that all PDF submissions should be uploaded at the Application PDF Upload site.  Please go to Form B to find which documents should be uploaded onto the Application PDF Upload site.  

NOTE:
PDF files  4 megabytes (4MB) or larger often cause problems in the upload process.  If your file does exceed 4MB, please contact the Office of Information Management (OIM) before attempting to upload the file.  OIM will provide you with technical advice and assistance in this process.   Contact e-mail and telephone numbers are: woldem@lsc.gov phone 202-295-1504; jordanr@lsc.gov phone 202-295-1508.  

PDF Submission Instructions:  

1. Access the AIN website at www.ain.lsc.gov.
2. Click on the link, "Grant Application".
3. Then, click "Grant Application Forms".
4. Enter your Applicant ID and Password that you used to submit your Notice of Intent to Compete.
5. Click on "Application PDF Upload".
6. Click the appropriate upload document link.
7. Then click on "browse" to search for the saved PDF document (e.g. "111000Form I.pdf").
8. Once the correct document is chosen, click on the upload button, which will automatically submit the document to LSC.

LSC Receipt Acknowledgment:  

LSC will send applicants an acknowledgement immediately upon receipt of the electronic documents. Applicants should submit questions about this matter to the LSC Competition Service Desk at competition@lsc.gov.
