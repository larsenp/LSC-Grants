FY 2003 Competition: Correction Notice

The following is a correction to the Notice of Funds Availability published in Appendix A of the LSC 2003 Request for Proposal, and on Monday, April 22, 2002 in the Federal Register.

These documents stated that service areas competed in New Jersey and in Michigan for calendar year 2003 funding will be published during the week of June 17, 2002. 

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) has postponed the publication of Michigan service areas pending state planning decisions.  We anticipate that this information will be published in approximately three weeks - around the week of July 8, 2002.  At that time, LSC will also publish the dates for filing the Notice of Intent to Compete (NIC) and for filing grant applications for service areas in Michigan.   

LSC will publish the New Jersey service areas during the week of June 17, 2002, as originally noticed.  The deadline for filing the NIC for service areas in New Jersey is July 12, 2002.  The deadline for filing grant applications is August 9, 2002.

We hope that this correction does not create an inconvenience. Please contact the LSC service desk at 1.877.378.9997 or by e-mail at competition@lsc.gov, if you have any questions regarding this correction notice.

Date of this Notice: June 18, 2002
