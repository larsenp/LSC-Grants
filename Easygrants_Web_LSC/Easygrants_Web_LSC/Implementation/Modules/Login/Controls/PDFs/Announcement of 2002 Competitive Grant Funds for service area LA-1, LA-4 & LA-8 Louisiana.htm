ANNOUNCEMENT OF 2002 COMPETITIVE GRANT FUNDS
FOR SERVICE AREA LA-1, LA-4 and LA-8 IN LOUISIANA

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) is announcing the availability of 2002 competitive grant funds for service areas LA-1, LA-4 and LA-8 in Louisiana to provide civil legal services to LSC-eligible clients. The grant term will begin as soon as March 1, 2002.

The chart below shows the service area, annualized funding, poverty population, and the counties/populations that will be served under the grant.

LSC is soliciting grant proposals from interested parties who are qualified to provide effective, efficient and high quality civil legal services to eligible clients for service areas LA-1, LA-4 and LA-8 in Louisiana. Nonprofit organizations that have as a purpose the furnishing of legal assistance to eligible clients, private attorneys, groups of private attorneys or law firms, state or local governments, and substate regional planning and coordination agencies are eligible to apply.

The Request for Proposals (RFP) is available at www.ain.lsc.gov.  Pursuant to the LSC regulation governing competition (45 CFR 1634), a Notice of Intent to Compete (NIC) must be filed. The due date for filing the NIC is December 28, 2001 5:00 p.m. ET. Responses to the RFP are due January 21, 2002, 5:00 p.m. ET.

Applicants should disregard the service area listing for the LA-9 service area of Louisiana in Appendix-A of the 2002 LSC RFP.  LSC has canceled the competition and rejected all previous bids for Louisiana service area LA-9.
