45 C.F.R. § 1617.4

Recordkeeping

1. Pursuant to 45 C.F.R. § 1617.4, the recipient shall maintain written policies and procedures to guide its staff in complying with 45 C.F.R. Part 1617.
2. Maintain a list of class action cases where the recipient or recipient attorney is attorney of record and is involved in non-adversarial activities. Such list should contain the following information:
  • case name;
  • court; and
  • status of the case, including description of the non-adversarial activities.
