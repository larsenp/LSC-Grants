            LSC is soliciting grant proposals from interested parties who are qualified to provide effective, efficient and high quality civil legal services to eligible clients for service areas in Michigan.  Nonprofit organizations that have as a purpose the furnishing of legal assistance to eligible clients, private attorneys, groups of private attorneys or law firms, state or local governments, and substate regional planning and coordination agencies are eligible to apply. 

The Request for Proposals (RFP) is available at www.ain.lsc.gov.  Pursuant to the LSC regulation governing competition (45 CFR 1634), a Notice of Intent to Compete (NIC) must be filed.  The due date for filing the NIC is July 30, 2001.  Responses to the RFP are due September 4, 2001, 5:00 p.m. EDT.  Applicants should disregard the service area listing for Michigan in Appendix-A of the 2002 LSC RFP.   LSC has canceled the competition and rejected all bids for Michigan service areas MI-12, MI-13, MI-14, and MI-15.  If you have questions regarding the RFP or this notice, please contact Reginald J. Haley (202.336.8827, e-mail haleyr@lsc.gov), or Jennifer Bateman (202.336.8835, e-mail batemanj@lsc.gov).
