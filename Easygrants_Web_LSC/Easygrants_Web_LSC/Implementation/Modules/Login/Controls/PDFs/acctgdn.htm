Accounting Guide for LSC Recipients

Updated: 15 February 2005

The Legal Services Corporation Office of Program Operations has released a new Accounting Guide for LSC Recipients. This Guide is effective August 14, 1997, and replaces the accounting portions of the 1981 and 1986 editions of LSC Audit and Accounting Guide for Recipients and Auditors (Audit and Accounting Guide). Now that this Guide has been published in final, all previous editions of the Audit and Accounting Guide are obsolete.

The Corporation may periodically make revisions to this Guide. LSC recipients and their auditors should keep their copies of the Guide current, incorporating all revisions to the Guide upon receipt. It is the recipient's responsibility to furnish copies of the current guide, and revisions thereto, to its auditors.
