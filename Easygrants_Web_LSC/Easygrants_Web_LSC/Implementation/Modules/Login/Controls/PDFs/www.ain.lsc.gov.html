Welcome to the Applicant Information Network. 

This site is for the use of parties who are interested in applying for grants from the Legal Services Corporation.  Information which applicants submit through this site is confidential and may not be distributed or discussed without the consent of the Legal Services Corporation and the applicant submitting the information. Click on Bulletin Board to access the LSC Request for Proposals and other information about applying for LSC grants.
