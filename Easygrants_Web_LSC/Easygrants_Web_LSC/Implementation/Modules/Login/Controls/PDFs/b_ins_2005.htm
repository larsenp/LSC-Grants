LSC Year 2005 Grants Competition

Instructions for Application Checklist -- Form B

This section contains instructions for the Grant Application Checklist -- Form B.

 

The LSC Grant Application Forms are accessible for all applicants who have submitted the Notice of Intent to Compete (Form H).  To access the grant application forms, click Grant Applications from the Legal Services Corporation Applicant Information Network homepage (www.ain.lsc.gov). This will take you to the Competitive Grant Applications menu. Then select Grant Application Forms and enter the LSC Applicant ID and the Password you used for submitting the Notice of Intent to Compete form.

After you have entered your Applicant ID and password, you will access the Grant Application Forms Menu. Select Checklist - Form B and click 'OK' to continue.

Form B

Form B is the Grant Application Checklist and needs to be completed after completion of the other forms and materials in the grant application package. There are three sections to Form B: PDF documents transmitted to: PDFsubmissions@lsc.gov, electronic documents transmitted using the AIN (Applicant Information Network), and electronic forms transmitted using the AIN. Enter the numbers of pages of each of the PDF documents sent to PDFSubmissions@lsc.gov. Also enter the numbers of pages of the electronic documents uploaded to LSC as part of Form L.

The number of pages for the electronic forms will appear automatically as you submit each form. If all required documents have been submitted, 'Yes' will appear in the right column. If 'No' appears, one or more required forms have not been submitted.  Because Form B tracks submission of all forms, please submit this form after you have submitted all the other required forms.

To save, exit or submit, use the buttons at the bottom of the screen.

Please note that all PDF submissions should be sent by email to PDFsubmissions@lsc.gov. Please go to Form B to find which documents should be transmitted to PDFsubmissions@lsc.gov

 

PDF Submission Instructions:

1.      Submit each document as a separate e-mail.

2.      Identify the grantee number and the document submitted on the subject line of the email. (e.g. “Grantee 100000 - Form I”)

3.      Do not submit paper copies to LSC.

 

LSC Receipt Acknowledgment:  

LSC will send applicants an acknowledgement immediately upon receipt of the electronic documents. Applicants should submit questions about this matter to the LSC Competition Service Desk at competition@lsc.gov.  Do not submit inquiries to PDFsubmissions@lsc.gov.
