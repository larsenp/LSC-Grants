Legal Services Corporation

 


MEMORANDUM


TO: ALL INTERESTED PARTIES

 
FROM: Office of Program Performance

 
RE: FY 2002 Request for Proposals

 
DATE: April 12, 2001

During the month of April 2001, LSC will begin the FY 2002 grants competition activities with the publication of service areas in competition and the publication of the RFP (Request for Proposals). This e-mail memorandum is being forwarded to all current LSC grantees to remind them of key competition and grant renewal activities and dates. In accordance with LSC’s multi-year funding policy, the solicitation of grant proposals will only be for specified service areas.

Please note:

1 Service areas that are subject to FY 2002 grants competition will be published during the week of April 16 at www.ain.lsc.gov , and in the Federal Register.


2 The FY 2002 RFP will be available from the LSC web site at www.ain.lsc.gov during the week of April 23, 2001.
 

As in previous years, LSC requires applicants to submit several grant documents electronically using the LSC Internet Applicant Information Network (AIN). Information submitted through AIN is secure from unauthorized interception. Instructions concerning the use of AIN will be included in the RFP.

Beginning April 16, applicants may submit inquiries to LSC regarding the grants competition by fax at 1-887-378-9997, or by e-mail at competition@lsc.gov .

LSC invites all interested parties to participate in the calendar year 2002 grants competition. Applicants are requested to visit the LSC web site at www.ain.lsc.gov regularly to remain informed about the FY 2002 grants competition.

Please note: Applicants that received a three-year grant beginning in calendar year 2000, or a two- or three-year grant beginning in calendar year 2001 are required to file a grant renewal application.
