LSC Announces Participants for Spring Conference

          LSC is pleased to announce the list of participants for the upcoming LSC-sponsored conference, "Creating Client-Centered State Communities of Justice." The conference will be held at the Hotel Hershey in Hershey, Pennsylvania from April 25-28, 2001. The purpose of the conference is to bring together active client/community advocates and legal services staff who embrace the concept of "client-centered legal services" and provide a forum for the exchange of ideas, thoughts, plans and strategies about how the legal services community can center its work in terms of what empowers, strengthens and enhances the lives of our clients.
