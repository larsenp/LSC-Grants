OFFICE OF GOVERNMENTAL RELATIONS AND PUBLIC AFFAIRS

MEMORANDUM

TO: The Field
 
FROM: Mauricio Vivero, Vice President

Catherine Sulzer, Deputy Director
 
DATE: December 22, 2000
 
SUBJECT

FY 2001 Appropriation

December 21, 2000 – President Clinton has signed into the law the last of the remaining appropriations measures, including the FY01 Commerce, Justice, State and Judiciary Appropriations bill (H.R. 4942). Legal Services Corporation will receive almost $330 million in funding, an increase just under $25 million from last year. This increase will likely translate into an estimated 7.18 percent increase in funds for the field.

Congress had originally passed H.R. 4942 in October, granting LSC an even increase of $25 million. Later budget negotiations, however, resulted in a .22 percent across the board cut for FY2001 spending.
