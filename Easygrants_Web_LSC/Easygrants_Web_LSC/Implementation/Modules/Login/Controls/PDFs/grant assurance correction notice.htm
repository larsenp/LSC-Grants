FY 2004 Competition: Correction Notice

The following is a correction to the date that Form-C (the 2004 LSC Grant Assurances)  will be available from www.ain.lsc.gov.

The original date for publishing the 2004 LSC Grant Assurances was the week of June 2, 2003 .  That date is changed.  LSC anticipates that the 2004 Grant Assurances will be available by or before the end of July 2003.  Applicants are asked to access Form-C at that time. 

Please note that starting this year, applicants will use Form-I (LSC Certification Form) to certify that they have reviewed and will comply with the LSC Grant Assurances.  As a result, applicants are requested to submit Form-I to LSC after Form-C is published at www.ain.lsc.gov.  Applicants will be required to submit Form-I within ten calendar-days after Form-C is published.  Please do not return the LSC Grant Assurances to LSC.

We hope that this correction does not create an inconvenience. Please contact the LSC service desk at competition@lsc.gov, if you have any questions regarding this matter.

Date of this Notice: June 5, 2003

Please visit this site regularly to remain current on the LSC competitive grants process for FY 2004.
