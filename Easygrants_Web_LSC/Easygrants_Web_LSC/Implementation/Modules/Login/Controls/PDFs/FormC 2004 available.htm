The LSC 2004 Grant Assurances (Form-C) are now available. Click here to access Form-C.

Please note that starting this year, applicants will use Form-I (LSC Certification Form) to certify that they have reviewed and will comply with the LSC Grant Assurances. As a result, applicants are requested to submit Form-I to LSC after Form-C is published. Applicants are required to submit Form-I within ten calendar-days after Form-C is published. Applicants should not return the LSC Grant Assurances to LSC.
