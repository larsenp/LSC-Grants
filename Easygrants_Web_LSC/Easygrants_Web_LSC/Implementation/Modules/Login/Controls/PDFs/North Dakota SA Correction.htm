FY 2003 Competition: Correction Notice

The following is a correction to the service area description for NND-3 in North Dakota published in Appendix A of the calendar year 2003 Request for Proposals.  The original description was “Native Americans living in all counties in the state of North Dakota.”

The correct description for service area NND-3 in North Dakota is:

Native Americans living in all counties in North Dakota, including those who live on the Devils Lake, Turtle Mountain, and Spirit Lake Reservations (formerly known as Fort Totten Reservation), and Native Americans living on the Fort Berthold Indian Reservation in the counties of Mountrail, Dunn, McKenzie, McLean and Mercer.

We hope that this correction does not create an inconvenience. Please contact Reginald Haley, Office of Program Performance at 202.336.8827 or by e-mail at haleyr@lsc.gov , if you have any questions regarding this matter.

 

Date of this Notice: April 29, 2002.
