FY2002 Competition: Correction Notice

The following is a correction to the Notice of Funds Availability published Thursday, April 19, 2001 in the Fereal Register:

The VA-16 service area of Virginia is not now in competiion for FY 2002 funding. The VA-16 service area comprises the following counties: Accomack, Gloucester, James City, Mathews, Middlesex, Northampton, York, Chesapeake City, Hampton City, Newport News City, Norfolk City, Poquoson City, Portsmouth City, Virginia Beach City, Williamsburg City.

We hope that this correction does not create an inconvenience. Please contact the LSC service desk at 1.877.378.9997 or by e-mail at competition@lsc.gov, if you have any questions regarding this matter.

Date of this Notice: April 30, 2001
