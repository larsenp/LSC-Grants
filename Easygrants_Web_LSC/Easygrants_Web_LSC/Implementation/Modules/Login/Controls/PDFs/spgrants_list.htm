Legal Services Corporation

State Planning Technical Assistance Grant Awards

State

Applicant

Amount

Purpose

California

San Francisco Neighborhood Legal Assistance Foundation on behalf of the San Francisco Bay Area Working Group (the Bay Area programs)

$15,000

To help pay consultant costs to develop a comprehensive, integrated delivery system for the San Francisco Bay Area. Matching funds to be provided by Bay Area programs.

California

Legal Aid Society of Orange County on behalf of all five LSC-funded programs in the Los Angeles Area

$10,000

To help defray the cost of an engineer consultant to assist in the planning and implementation of a region-wide intake/service system to unify the access process for all residents of the Los Angeles-Orange County service area.

Colorado

Legal Aid Society of Metropolitan Denver on behalf of all three Colorado LSC programs, the Legal Aid Foundation of Colorado and the Statewide Planning Group

$15,000

To assist with consultant and other costs to facilitate merger of the three programs into a single statewide program by January 1, 2000. A $5,000 match to be provided by the Legal Aid Foundation of Colorado.

Louisiana

Louisiana State Bar Association

$9,500

To pay for a consultant to develop and help implement a state technology plan to improve integrated delivery of client services. The consultant will work with all LSC programs and the Technology Subcommittee of the State Bar Access to Justice Program which has been coordinating Louisiana's technology planning.

Maine

Pine Tree Legal Assistance on behalf of the "State Planning Legal Services Response Team"

$5,000

To help underwrite the creation of a shared Internet site for poverty law case handlers, including pro bono attorneys. The site will post a brief bank of significant pleadings organized by subject matter and a complete set of Maine District Court forms that could be completed on line and printed for filing. Matching funds of $2,500 to be provided by Maine Bar Foundation.

Mississippi

East Mississippi Legal Services on behalf of all six Mississippi LSC funded Programs

$15,000

To enhance statewide resource development strategies. Matching funds contributed by all six Mississippi programs.

Nebraska

Nebraska State Bar Association

$10,500

To help with expenses related to the formation of a statewide entity, statewide technology planning, and statewide intake & advice system. Matching funds of up to $25,000 to be provided by the Nebraska State Bar Foundation.

New Hampshire

Legal Advice and Referral Center

$7,500

To pay for a consultant and other costs to facilitate a series of state planning meetings involving a variety of state stakeholders, including staff and Board of LSC and non-LSC programs, the New Hampshire Bar Association and New Hampshire Bar Foundation. Matching funds of $6,000 to be provided by the New Hampshire Bar Foundation.

New Mexico

State Bar of New Mexico

$7,500

To hire a consultant and assist with the costs of two statewide meetings of stakeholders to advance the implementation of New Mexico's state plan.

New York

Southern Tier Legal Services on behalf of all participants in the NY State Planning Process

$15,000

Sharing in the costs of New York's first ever statewide Intake Conference in the fall, 1999. The conference is one implementing piece of the overall collaborative statewide plan, as set out in the Planning for Enhanced Outcomes -- 1998: Strengthening Civil Legal Services in New York. Matching funds to be provided by the New York IOLA Fund.

North Carolina

North Central Legal Assistance Program on behalf of all four North Carolina LSC grantees

$15,000

To pay for facilitation and other planning expenses to further develop an integrated statewide legal services delivery system. Matching funds to be provided by the North Carolina IOLTA Program.

Ohio

Ohio Legal Assistance Foundation

$12,500

To provide merger consulting services to programs to implement the reconfiguration called for by the Ohio State Plan. Matching funds to be provided by the Ohio Legal Assistance Foundation.

Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania Legal Services on behalf all participants in the Pennsylvania State Planning Process

$12,500

To pay for consultants to provide management and technical assistance to the Statewide Legal Services Steering Committee. Matching funds to be provided by Pennsylvania IOLTA Program.
