Legal Services Corporation

Competition 2001 and Grant Renewal Information

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) awards grants for the provision of civil legal services to low income persons.

Eligible applicants for LSC funding include nonprofit organizations that have as a purpose the provision of legal assistance to eligible clients, private attorneys, groups of private attorneys or law firms, state or local governments, and substate regional planning and coordination agencies.

LSC funding is awarded through a system of competition, pursuant to LSC's competitive bidding regulation, 45 C.F.R. §1634, and LSC's appropriations act. Access the list of competed service areas at www.ain.lsc.gov, RFP Appendices (Appendix – A).
LSC's application review process is designed to evaluate each applicant's proposed legal services delivery approach, as measured against widely recognized quality standards, such as the American Bar Association Standards for the Providers of Civil Legal Services to the Poor and the LSC Performance Criteria. LSC's selection criteria are detailed in 45 C.F.R. §1634.9. Pursuant to the statute and regulation, LSC's competitive review process prohibits preference to current or previous recipients of LSC funds.

Notice of the availability of LSC funds is published in the Federal Register and sent to state bar associations, newspapers, law schools, recipients of LSC funding, state and local governments, substate regional planning and coordination agencies, and others.
To apply for funding, applicants must submit a Notice of Intent to Compete, and submit a complete application responsive to the RFP. The deadlines for FY 2001 funding appear below.

Information updates and changes regarding Competition 2001 and grant renewals will be posted on the LSC website at www.ain.lsc.gov.   Applicants and current grantees are requested to visit the LSC website regularly to remain informed about Competition 2001 and grant renewal activities.
LSC will establish a service desk to respond to requests for information and inquiries about Competition 2001 and grant renewals. The service desk will begin operation April 24, 2000. Contact the service desk by e-mail at competition@lsc.gov, or by fax at 1-877-378-9997. Responses to inquiries will be returned by fax or by e-mail. All inquiries should include the name of the individual and/or organization requesting information, a telephone number, fax number, and e-mail address, and the date of the request. Address all inquires to the attention of: LSC Competition Service Desk.
