LEGAL SERVICES CORPORATION
FY2001 GRANT AWARD DECISIONS

The chart below identifies the LSC grant award decisions for FY 2001.  The chart is organized by service area code for each state.  The grant award amounts shown in the chart have not been adjusted to reflect the anticipated FY 2001 appropriations increase.  The amount of the anticipated increase is approximately $20 million or 6.9% for the delivery of legal services.

The final grant award amounts (including any appropriations increase) and the length of the grant term will be addressed in the individual grant award letters.  Grant award letters will be distributed by December 22, 2000.

Awards will be made so that each service area is funded.  However, the organizations listed below are not guaranteed an award or contract.  LSC reserves the right to make changes to the information shown in the chart below.  

Grants and contracts are awarded under the authority conferred on LSC by the Legal Services Corporation Act, as amended (42 U.S.C. 2996e(a)(I)).

For further information contact:  Reginald Haley, Office of Program Performance, at (202) 336-8827, or by e-mail at haleyr@lsc.gov.
