Appendix H

MEMORANDUM

TO: ALL INTERESTED PARTIES 

 
FROM: LSC Office of Program Performance

 
RE: Applicants' Informational Session 

 
DATE: April 2003

 

          The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) will conduct a telephonic conference on May 22, 2003 , regarding the LSC Grants Competition. The conference is scheduled to last approximately two hours, depending on the number of questions received from participants. All interested parties are encouraged to participate. The objective is to provide an understanding of the information needed to prepare the competitive grant application and to promote participation in the competitive grant process.

Registration:

Participants should register for the conference by May 9, 2003 . A registration fax sheet is included in Appendix H of the RFP. If you plan to participate, complete the registration sheet and fax it to LSC at 202.336.7272. As part of the registration process, participants are requested to provide no more than five questions pertaining to the RFP, to be answered by panel members during the conference. Before submitting your questions, review the listing of responses to Frequently Asked Questions (included at Appendix F) to avoid submitting questions for which answers have already been provided. 

LSC will use a panel discussion format to address questions and issues pertaining to the RFP. This format allows the conference panel to organize and respond to participants' inquiries. Questions submitted with your registration will be responded to by the panel members.

During the telephonic conference, additional questions may be submitted to the panel by fax. To the extent possible, these additional questions will be answered during the conference. You may use your Registration Form for faxing additional questions. Prior to submitting your registration, please make copies of the form if necessary.

Conference Questionnaire:

A brief questionnaire is included in Appendix H. Its purpose is to allow you to evaluate the effectiveness of this conference. Please fax your completed questionnaire at the conclusion of the conference.

          Instructions for participating in the informational session will be made available to applicants upon receipt of their registration.
