Overview

As an independent corporation created by Congress and charged with distributing government funds, LSC is governed by federal law. Federal law also applies to recipients of LSC grant funds. The LSC Act, LSC Appropriations Statutes and LSC Regulations provide guidance on the operation and responsibilities of LSC and its grantees.
