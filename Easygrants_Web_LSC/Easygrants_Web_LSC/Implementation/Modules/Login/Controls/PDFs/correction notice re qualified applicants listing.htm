FY 2003 Competition: Correction Notice

The following is a correction to the "2003 Competition and Renewal Information" notice posted on the AIN. 

That notice stated that LSC would publish the listing of applicants that filed a qualified grant proposal for calendar year 2003 funding on September 9, 2002.  That date has been changed. 

LSC will publish the listing during the week of October 7, 2002.  The change in the publication date will permit LSC to incorporate funding estimates that are based on 2000 Census data.

We hope that this correction does not create an inconvenience. Please contact Reginald Haley, Office of Program Performance at 202.336.8827 or by e-mail at haleyr@lsc.gov , if you have any questions regarding this matter.

 

Date of this Notice: September 12, 2002.
