FY 2003 Competition: Correction Notice

The following is a correction to the Notice of Funds Availability published Monday, April 22, 2002 in the Federal Register.

The Federal Register contained incorrect service area codes for the state of Louisiana.  The correct service area codes for the state of Louisiana are LA-1 and LA-12. 

Service area LA-1 covers Ascension, Assumption, East Baton Rouge, East Feliciana, Iberville, Lafourche, Pointe Coupee, St. James, St. John the Baptist, Terrebonne, West Baton Rouge, and West Feliciana counties.

Service area LA-12 covers Jefferson, Livingston, Orleans, Plaquemines, St. Bernard, St. Charles, St. Helena, St. Tammany, Tangipahoa, and Washington counties.

We hope that this correction does not create an inconvenience. Please contact the LSC service desk at 1.877.378.9997 or by e-mail at competition@lsc.gov, if you have any questions regarding this matter.

Date of this Notice: May 3, 2002
