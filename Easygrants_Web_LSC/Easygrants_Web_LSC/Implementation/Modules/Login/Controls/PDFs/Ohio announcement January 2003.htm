ANNOUNCEMENT OF 2003 COMPETITIVE GRANT FUNDS
FOR SERVICE AREAS IN OHIO

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) is announcing the availability of 2003 competitive grant funds for service area OH-19 in Ohio to provide civil legal services to LSC-eligible clients.  The grant term begins July 1, 2003 .   

The exact amount of congressional appropriated funds and the date and terms of their availability are not yet known, but are expected to be similar to 2002 funding.

LSC is soliciting grant proposals from interested parties who are qualified to provide effective, efficient and high quality civil legal services to eligible clients for service areas in Ohio .  Nonprofit organizations that have as a purpose the furnishing of legal assistance to eligible clients, private attorneys, groups of private attorneys or law firms, state or local governments, and substate regional planning and coordination agencies are eligible to apply.  

The Request for Proposals (RFP) is available at www.ain.lsc.gov.  Pursuant to the LSC regulation governing competition (45 CFR 1634), a Notice of Intent to Compete (NIC) must be filed.  The due date for filing the NIC is February 14, 2003 5:00 p.m. ET .  Responses to the RFP are due March 14, 2003 , 5:00 p.m. ET .   

If you have questions regarding the RFP or this notice, please contact Reginald J. Haley (202.336.8827, e-mail haleyr@lsc.gov), or Mounia Bensalah (202.336.8914, e-mail bensalahm@lsc.gov).
