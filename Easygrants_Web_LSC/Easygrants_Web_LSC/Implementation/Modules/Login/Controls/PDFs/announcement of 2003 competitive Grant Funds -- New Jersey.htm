Addendum to Appendix A of the 2003 LSC Request for Proposals
ANNOUNCEMENT OF 2003 COMPETITIVE GRANT FUNDS
FOR SERVICE AREAS MNJ, NJ-8, NJ-12, NJ-15, NJ-16, NJ-17, and NJ-18

          The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) announces the proposed availability of competitive grant funds to provide civil legal services to eligible clients during calendar year 2003 for service areas MNJ, NJ-8, NJ-12, NJ-15, NJ-16, NJ-17, and NJ-18 in New Jersey.

          LSC is soliciting grant proposals from interested parties who are qualified to provide effective, efficient, and high quality civil legal services to eligible clients. Nonprofit organizations that have as a purpose the furnishing of legal assistance to eligible clients, private attorneys, groups of private attorneys or law firms, state or local governments, and substate regional planning and coordination agencies are eligible to apply.

          The Request for Proposals (RFP) is available at www.ain.lsc.gov. Descriptions of the New Jersey service areas are below.

Note: The estimated grant amounts listed below are based on the FY 2002 LSC Appropriation and grant award distributions. These amounts are likely to be substantially affected by the 2000 census data.The census data on poverty population used to calculate estimated grant amounts were not available at the time of this publication. LSC will make every effort to inform applicants in a timely manner of changes to the grant amounts based on the 2000 census.

          Pursuant to the LSC regulation governing competition (45 CFR 1634), a Notice of Intent to Compete (NIC) must be filed. The due date for filing the NIC for service areas in New Jersey is July 12, 2002, 5:00 p.m. EDT. The due date for filing grant proposals for service areas in New Jersey is August 9, 2002, 5:00 p.m. EDT.

          Applicants are encouraged to visit the LSC website at www.ain.lsc.gov regularly to remain current on updates to the LSC competitive grants process including the effects of the 2000 census.

          Please contact the LSC competition service desk at by fax at 1.877.378.9997, or by e-mail at competition@lsc.gov, if you have any questions regarding this matter.
