CFR 45 Part 1600 et. seq.

Notice

These regulations files are posted for the convenience of Legal Services Corporation (LSC) employees, the staff of LSC-funded programs and other interested parties. We make efforts to see that these files are current and correct, but visitors are reminded that the authoritative source for Federal regulations are the U.S. Government Printing Office and the Office of the Federal Register, National Archives and Records Administration. The Code of Federal Regulations and the Federal Register may be accessed through GPOAccess online at: http://www.gpoaccess.gov/nara/index.html

Please report any errors in this volume to the Office of the General Counsel so that appropriate corrections may be made.
