Form G-6: Self-Inspection Summary

 Top 

Form G-6: Self-Inspection captures information on the percentage of sampled CSR cases that have reporting errors, what types of errors occurred, and how many non-LSC funded cases were closed that were not reported to LSC. Form G-6 contains three sections: Certification, Summary and Unreported Non-LSC Cases. These sections are attached to Program Letter 11-2, as are the Case Review Form and the Sample Selection Procedure.

 

The Self-Inspection procedure is set out in Program Letter 11-2. Once the Self-Inspection is completed, the results should be entered in this form.

 

For the Self-Inspection Summary section below, please enter the number of cases with each of the 12 types of exceptions in the boxes under Numbers of Cases (if there are no instances of an exception, you may enter 0 or leave it blank. Also check the box in the corrective action column if corrective action was taken in any of these 12 types of exceptions.

 

Please note the following:

 

•To save data, click Save and Calculate/Save.
•To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.

 

Follow the steps below for assistance on this form:
