The Submission section indicates whether you are able to submit your Notice of Intent to Compete application. If all pages in the application have been completed, click Submit.

 

In the View Full Submission section, click View PDF to view a PDF of your Notice of Intent to Compete application.

 

The Validation Summary section displays the status of your submission, indicating whether each section is validated (complete). Click the section links below or use the links on the left to navigate through the sections of the application.
