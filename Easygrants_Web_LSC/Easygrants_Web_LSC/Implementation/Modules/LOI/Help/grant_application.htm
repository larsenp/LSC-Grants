The LSC Request for Proposals (RFP) is available from www.grants.lsc.gov . Applicants must complete and submit grant applications, following the requirements of the RFP, using this website. See "Key Competition and Renewal Dates" - at www.grants.lsc.gov (Announcements and Updates) for assistance, deadlines regarding publication dates and filing deadlines.

 

In order to submit a grant application, an Applicant must first submit a Notice of Intent to Compete (NIC) to LSC. LSC requires Applicants to submit the NIC using this website.

 

Note: LSC annually holds an Applicants’ Informational Session (AIS). This is a free webinar conference sponsored by LSC to assist Applicants in preparing the competitive grant application and to promote participation in the competitive grants process. See details about the conference at www.grants.lsc.gov, “LSC Grants Announcements and Updates.”

 

Contact the LSC service desk at competition@lsc.gov if you have any questions regarding this notice.
