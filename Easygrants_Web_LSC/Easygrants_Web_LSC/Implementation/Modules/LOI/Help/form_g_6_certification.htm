Form G-6: Self-Inspection Certification

 Top 

Form G-6: Self-Inspection captures information on the percentage of sampled CSR cases that have reporting errors, what types of errors occurred, and how many non-LSC funded cases were closed that were not reported to LSC. Form G-6 contains three sections: Certification, Summary and Unreported Non-LSC Cases. These sections are attached to Program Letter 11-2, as are the Case Review Form and the Sample Selection Procedure.

 

The Self-Inspection procedure is set out in Program Letter 11-2. Once the Self-Inspection is completed, the results should be entered in this form, just as they were entered in the hard copy Self-Inspection in prior years.

 

For the Self-Inspection Certification section below, please be certain to enter the number of cases sampled in item A (You must enter a number greater than 0 in this field) and enter the number of cases having one or more exceptions in the applicable fields in item B (You must enter a number in this field that is smaller than the number in item a; the number may be 0). Please answer the yes/no questions in items 1 and 2 of the Certification Section and if the answer to item 2 was yes, please enter the number of cases excluded from the CSR submission in the field at the end of Item 2. Please check any applicable boxes in item 3. The Executive Director’s name should be entered at the end of the Certification Section, but no signature is required.

 

Please note the following:

 

•To save data, click Save and Calculate/ Save.
•To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.
