Form J-1: Components of Actual PAI

 Top 

The Form J-1: Components of Actual PAI page captures information on actual calendar year 2011 private attorney involvement. Please include, for each type of private attorney involvement in which your program engages, actual numbers of cases referred and closed during 2011, as well as actual numbers of private attorneys who accepted referrals of cases and the number of attorneys who volunteered to participate. Programs with Migrant Farmworker and Native American service areas do not need to complete this form for those service areas.

 

Please note that the total number of "cases closed" in the 2nd column of Form J-1 should be equal to the total PAI cases closed reported on form G-3(d). If this number is not equal to the total of the G-3(d), you will not be able to submit the J-1 Form. In the event that you have a variance that you cannot easily reconcile, it is our view that the G-3(d) which is subject to the Self-Inspection process is more likely to be accurate and the J-1 Column 2 totals should be reconciled to the G-3(d).

 

Please note that LSC has made minor revisions to Form J-1 in order to more accurately separate Pro Bono and Compensated case numbers. The form is now divided into Pro Bono and Compensated groups. Categories such as Co-Counsel and Other cases that could be either Compensated or Pro Bono are given lines under each group. Also, we have entirely eliminated the Lawyer Referral Service category, since very few cases were closed under it. Should you have any such cases, please report them under Other, either compensated or pro Bono as is appropriate.
