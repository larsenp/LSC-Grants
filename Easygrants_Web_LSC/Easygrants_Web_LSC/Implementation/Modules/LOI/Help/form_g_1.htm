The Form G-1: Projected Case Services page captures information on projected calendar year 2011 case services. Please include information on all case services expected to be eligible to be supported with LSC funds, regardless of whether they were actually supported with any LSC funds.

 

Please do not include PAI case services in information on Basic Field-General service areas.

 

Click here for Projected Case Service definitions.
