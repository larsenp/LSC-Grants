Form G-4: Client Age, Ethnicity and Gender

 Top 

The Form G-4: Client Age, Ethnicity and Gender page captures information on the age, ethnicity and gender of LSC-eligible clients who have received LSC-eligible services during 2011, regardless of whether LSC funds were used to deliver these services.

 

Please include information on clients for whom your program closed cases during the year, as reported in Form G-3 and Form G-3(d). The G-4 Form should total all clients, whether served by staff or by PAI. Accordingly, the G-4 totals for Basic Field service areas should approximate the totals of G-3 plus G-3(d) for that service area; for Migrant and Native American service areas, they should be approximately equal to the G-3 form for that service area. Please note that this form also requests numbers of male and female clients.
