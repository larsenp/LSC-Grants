Form G-3(d): Actual Case Services (PAI)

 Top 

The Form G-3: Actual Case Services (Staff) page captures information on actual case closures during calendar year 2011. Cases are to be reported by service area. Please note that the 2008 Edition of the CSR Handbook applies to the submission of CSR information contained in Form G-3. Click here to view the 2008 CSR Handbook. For further information, you can check the Frequently Asked Questions at http://grants.lsc.gov/rin under Grantee Guidance, Case Service Reporting (CSR).

 

All G-3 forms, except PAI, are denominated as Form G-3: Actual Case Services (Staff) on their top line. There is an indication on the second line whether they are G-3(a) for Basic Field, G-3(b) for Migrant or G-3(c) for Native American. Since that is the only visible difference, it is important to check that second line from the top before making entries, to ensure that you are entering the data for the right service area.

 

Please report all closed cases which were eligible to have been supported with LSC funds, regardless of whether they were actually supported with any LSC funds. Please report Basic Field PAI cases only on Form G-3(d) and do not include them in your Basic Field case closures in Form G-3. There are no G-3(d) forms for Migrant or Native American service areas. All cases closed with Migrant or Native American funds must be reported on the G-3(b) for Migrant or G-3(c) for Native American, even if a few are closed by private attorneys (see 2008 CSR Handbook §4.2).

 

In order for these forms to save properly, every cell must contain either zero or an integer number of cases or be left blank. Blank entries will count as zeroes. Entry of non-numeric characters (commas, decimal points, letters, etc.) will result in not allowing your form to be saved and you will find in an error message at the top of your form which must be corrected before you can save. We recommend clicking Save and Calculate frequently to assure saving your work and so you can easily find any errors you may have made. After completing your entries of cases closed, it is essential to click Save and Calculate or Save and Continue to save what you have entered. Please remember to respond to the questions at the top of the G-3 form before submitting the form.
