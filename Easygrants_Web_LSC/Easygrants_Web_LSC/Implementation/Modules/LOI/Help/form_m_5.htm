Form M: Indirect Other Services

 Top  Previous  Next

The Form M-5: Indirect Other Services page is designed to capture information on non-case activities that address legal problems of the low income community through models that supplement and complement traditional, more direct legal assistance activities. This includes legal education for lay service providers and collaborative service delivery models. Please check all applicable methods used. Please also describe in your narrative the models that you listed under "Miscellaneous Other Indirect Services". Note: Check only the methods used during the reporting period.

 

Please note the following:

 

•To save data, click Save and Calculate.
•To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.
