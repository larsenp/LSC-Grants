Submission Requirements

 

All D Forms and the G-3, G-4 and G-5 Forms must be submitted separately for each service area. G-3(d), and J-1 are submitted only for Basic Field Service areas. All E Forms (Staffing), the G-6 Form (Self-Inspection) and Forms M and L are submitted only once for each grantee.

 

Due Dates

 

The 2013 year end Grant Activity Reports are due according to the following schedule:

 

February 18

Case Service Information (Actual) and CSR Self Inspection, Other Case, and PAI Information

(Forms G-2, G-3,- G-3(d), G-4, G-5, G-5(d), G-6, J-1)

March 3

Staffing, Budget

(Forms E-0, E-1, D-1, D-3)

 

Other Services Reports

(Forms M and Form L, Narrative)

 

All programs receiving 2013 LSC funding must submit:

 

  1. Actual Grant Activity Reports

  2. Other Services Reports and CSR Self-Inspection Reports

  3. Office and Staffing Information
