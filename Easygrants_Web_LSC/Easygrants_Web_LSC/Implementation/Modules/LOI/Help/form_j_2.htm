Form J-2: Projected Components of PAI

 Top  Previous  Next

The Form J-2: Components of Projected PAI page captures information on projected calendar year 2011 private attorney involvement. Please include projected numbers of cases referred and closed, as well as projected numbers of private attorneys either volunteering to participate and/or actually accepting referrals of cases, for each type of private attorney involvement in which your program engages. Programs with Migrant Farmworker and Native American service areas do not need to submit this form for those service areas.

 

Please note the following:

 

•To save data, click Save and Calculate.
•To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.

 

Follow the steps below for assistance on this form:

 

•Submit inquiries pertaining to technical issues, using the website application, and uploading files to techsupport@lsc.gov.
