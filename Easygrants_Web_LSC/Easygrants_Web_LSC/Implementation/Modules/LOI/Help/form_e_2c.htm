Form E-2(c): Projected Staffing Subrecipient

 Top  Previous  Next

The Form E-2(c): Projected Staffing Subrecipient page captures information on projected subrecipient staffing levels as of December 31, 2010. Please include in this form information for all subrecipient offices combined, but do not include information on recipient main and branch offices. Information on recipient main and branch offices is reported separately in Form E-2(a). In this form you can enter whole numbers or numbers with one decimal place.
