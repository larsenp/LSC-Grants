Referred Referral Form M-3(a)

 

In Form M-3(a) please report referrals that do not qualify as 'cases' - i.e. any referral in which no actual legal advice or other legal service is provided directly to the client. See Referred Referral M-3(a) for the various categories included in this form.

 

In the Measured field please enter the number of other services that you are able to count. In the Estimated field, please enter any additional other services that you have not counted but have estimated on a reasonable basis. The total field is a calculated field which adds up the measured & estimated figures. The total number of other services on this form must be equal to the total number of people on Referred Referral Form M-3(b).

 

Referred Referral Form M-3(b)

 

In Form M-3(b) you need to enter the total number for each subject matter category without breakdown by measured and estimated. The total in this form must be equal to the total figure in the Referred Referral Form M-3(a).
