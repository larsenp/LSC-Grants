Actual Office and Staffing Information -- Forms E-0 & E-1

 

This section contains instructions on updating actual office and staffing information. All recipients of 2011 LSC funding must complete these forms.

 

Programs which submitted office and staffing information last year will find these forms pre-filled with last year's information and need only to update that information. Programs who did not submit office and staffing information last year will need to enter a complete set of staffing and office information. Programs that merged in the prior year will find all the surviving program's offices and staffing information pre-filled and we will also, where possible, pre-fill the non-surviving program(s) offices and their staff, renumbering them with numbers larger than the highest number used by the surviving program and redesignating their former main office as a branch.

 

Form E-0: Actual Office Information

 

This section contains instructions on updating the actual office information. Main office, branch office and subrecipient office information are accessed through this page. You can add new office, edit or delete existing offices.

 

To add a new branch or subrecipient office, click "Add Branch Office" or "Add Subrecipient Office" and enter the office information in the blank fields. Please note that the system automatically provides Office # for the new office you are entering. Make sure to save before exiting.

 

If you want to edit/update office information, click on the "Edit" button corresponding to that particular office, update the information as necessary and save the data. Please update the information on the screen to reflect the office's current address, telephone number, fax number, and person in charge. For telephone and fax numbers, use (xxx) xxx-xxxx format.

 

The Regular Full-Time Work Week (Number of Hours) in Form E-0 will usually be 40, but if your normal work week is less than 40 hours, you can enter your actual normal work week. Normal work week CANNOT be over 40 hours, even if most of your staff are FLSA Exempt employees who actually do work more than 40 hours. This Regular Full-Time work week is the number of hours your hourly (non-exempt) staff are expected to work in order to earn a full paycheck.

 

If you have two different full-time work weeks for a single office (e.g. Attorneys 40 hours, support staff 37.5 hours) as a few programs do, record the smaller number as your full-time work week for that office, so all full-time staff will show at 100% full time.

 

Then in the staff detail data entry screen for Form E-1a or E-1b (Staffing, Main Office or Staffing Branch Office), you will be asked to enter “hours per work week” for that staff member. The “hours per work week” should be the number of hours that person is scheduled to work (overtime is excluded for non-exempt staff) and time above the normal work week is excluded for exempt staff). If a staff person terminates part way through the year, this does not affect the “hours per work week”, nor is it affected if the staff member goes on leave.

 

Please note the following:

 

•    To save data, click Save and Calculate/ Save.
•    To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.
