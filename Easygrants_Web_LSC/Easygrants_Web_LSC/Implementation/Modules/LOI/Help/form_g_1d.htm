Form G-1(d): Projected Case Services (PAI)

 Top  Previous  Next

Programs which received 2011 LSC Basic Field-General funding must complete Form G-1(d): Projected Case Services PAI for projected cases to be closed by private attorneys. Programs with Migrant Farmworker and Native American service areas do not need to complete this form for those service areas.
