On the Form M: Outreach page please report all the methods used to inform potential clients about the availability of services for which they might be eligible. The following methods are given in the form.

 

  1. Informational notices published in print media

  2. TV spots or PSAs

  3. Radio spots or PSAs

  4. Newsletter(s) - external to program

  5. Referral agreements with other agencies

  6. "How to reach us" pages on web sites

  7. Other.

 

Please check all applicable methods you used. Also please explain in your narrative any method that you listed under Other. Note: Check only the methods used during the reporting period.
