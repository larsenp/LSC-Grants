Form D-3: Actual Support and Revenue

 Top 

The Form D-3: Actual Support and Revenue page captures information on actual calendar-year 2011 support and revenue. Please report LSC and non-LSC support and revenue separately for each service area. The information which you submit in this form does not need to be audited. If you do not have audited revenue information at the time when you complete this form, please use unaudited information.

 

Please note that this form will not save properly with non-numeric data such as letters or spaces. Commas and decimal points may be used but are not required. Leaving a numeric cell blank will result in a zero being saved for that value. If you get an error message when entering this information, please note the message on the screen and correct it accordingly.

 

Click here to view Support and Revenue Definitions. Please note that only funds received for provision of civil legal services should be reported. Also please note that the Basic Field line under LSC Funding sources is already filled out based on LSC funding records and should not be changed – and further note that it will not show up in the totals on this form until after you save the file.

 

 

Please note the following:

 

•To save data, click Save and Calculate/ Save.
•To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.

 

Follow the steps below for assistance on this form:

 

•Submit inquiries pertaining to technical issues, using the website application, and uploading files to techsupport@lsc.gov.
•Submit all other inquiries regarding this form or the grants process to the Office of Information Management at rin@lsc.gov.
