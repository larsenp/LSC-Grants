Required Internet Forms

 

All applicants for LSC grant funds must submit a Notice of Intent to Compete and Grant Application Forms A, B, D, F, G, and K via the Internet. Use of these forms requires Netscape Navigator Version 4.0 or higher, or Internet Explorer version 4.0 or higher.

 

Notice of Intent to Compete

 

In order to participate in the competition process, an applicant must submit a Notice of Intent to Compete to LSC.  LSC requires applicants to submit the Notice of Intent to Compete using this Internet site. The Notice of Intent to Compete requires electronic submission of the following information:

 

1.A brief description of the service area(s) applied for;
2.The names and resume information of the principals and key staff; and
3.The names and resume information of current or proposed governing or policy body members and their appointing organizations.

 

Any applicant, including a current recipient of LSC funds, that fails to submit a timely Notice of Intent to Compete will be ineligible for the current grant year competition. Applicants that are unable to use this Internet site due to extraordinary circumstances beyond their control must contact  LSC at competition@lsc.gov prior to the due date of the Notice of Intent to Compete. The Corporation may agree to extend the date for submission of that Notice in the event of extraordinary circumstances.
