Form L: Other Services Narrative

 Top 

Please prepare your narrative according to the instructions provided with our Program Letter 02-9 using a word processing software.  The narrative should be between 300-1000 words and it should provide an overview of the other services provided briefly addressing each type of service that you reported in the other services forms regardless of funding sources. Only one narrative is submitted per grantee, irrespective of the number of Service Areas served.

 

While Form L is unchanged, we note that if you there is information you want to convey to LSC that would have been reported in parts of Form M that have been discontinued, Form L affords you an opportunity to include such information in your GAR report. There is no requirement to report any such information in Form L, but it does give you the opportunity to so report any such information that you consider to be important.

 

The uploads listed in the Upload Checklist below are required for this application. To upload a document:

 

1.In the Upload Checklist section below, click to download the template/instructions associated with the document, if available, as a guide to create your upload.
2.Then when you are ready to upload the document, select the Type from the drop-down in the Uploads section below and click Select Upload Type. You will be then be taken to the Upload Editor page where you can upload your document.
3.Follow the instructions on the Upload Editor page to upload the document.
