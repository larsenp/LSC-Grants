Form G-5: Actual Open Cases (Staff)

 Top 

The Form G-5: Actual Open Cases (Staff) page captures by service area information on the total number of cases eligible to be supported with LSC funds which remained open at the end of 2011.  These cases include those which your program opened during the course of 2011, or in a year prior to 2011, and which remained open on December 31, 2011.

 

Please note the following:

 

•To save data, click Save and Calculate/ Save.
•To continue to the next page, click Save and Continue.

 

Follow the steps below for assistance on this form:

 

•Submit inquiries pertaining to technical issues, using the website application, and uploading files to techsupport@lsc.gov.
•Submit all other inquiries regarding this form or the grants process to the Office of Information Management at rin@lsc.gov.
