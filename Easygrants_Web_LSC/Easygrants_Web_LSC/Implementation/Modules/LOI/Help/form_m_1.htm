Form M: Community Legal Education

The Other Services forms are designed to collect information on non case activities (other services) as described in our Program Letter 02-9 dated October 16, 2002 which replaced Program Letter 01-2 dated May 15, 2002.  Before you start working on this form we urge you to go through the revised Program Letter 02-9 (including all the attachments), and Other Services Reports Frequently Asked Questions.  All recipients of 2009 funding should complete these forms.

 

There are six categories of non-case other services:

 

  1. Community Legal  Education

  2. Pro Se Assistance

  3. Referrals

  4. Outreach

  5. Indirect Other Services

  6. Miscellaneous Other services

 

Item I consists of 3 forms. Form I(A) captures the number of people directly receiving service by the "Models Used" and Form B shows the breakdown by "Type of Service".  Form C, however, doesn't measure the number of people as the other two forms. It captures the number of times that a community legal education item appeared in media that reach residents of the Program's service area.

 

There are two numeric forms for Items number II, III & VI. In the first set of forms, the (A) Forms, you need to enter the number of people directly receiving service by the "Models Used".  In the second set of forms, the (B) Forms, you enter the number of people by "Type of Service".  The total number in the 'A' forms must be equal to the total number on the corresponding 'B' forms. Please check carefully to make sure that these two totals are equal; if they are not equal, you will not be able to submit the form. In the event that you have a variance that you cannot easily reconcile, it is our view that the "A" forms are more likely to be accurate, so you should reconcile the "B" figures to the "A" figures (when you cannot accurately allocate "A" Other Services totals into the "B" categories, the remainder should be placed in "Other".

 

Items IV & V have one form each and capture non-numeric data.

 

Community Legal Education Form M-1(a)

 

In Form M-1(a) enter the number of people receiving service by models used. This consists of activities aimed at informing the community about their legal rights and responsibilities connected with various situations they might encounter using different ways as given in the form.

 

In the Measured field please enter the number of other services that you are able to count. In the Estimated field, please enter any additional other services that you have not counted but have estimated on a reasonable basis. The total field is a calculated field which adds up the measured & estimated figures. The total number of other services on this form must be equal to the total number on Community Legal Education Form M-1(b) which gives the breakdown by type of service. - See the bottom figure in Column (C).

 

Community Legal Education Form M-1(b)

 

In Form M-1(b) enter the total number for each subject matter category without breakdown by measured and estimated. The total in Form M-1(b) must be equal to the total figure in Community Legal Education Form M-1(a) - See the bottom figure in Column (C).

 

Community Legal Education Form M-1(c)

 

In Form M-1(c) enter the total number of times a community legal education item appeared in media that reach residents of the program's service area by models used.
