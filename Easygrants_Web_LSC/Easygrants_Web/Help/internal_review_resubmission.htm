Internal Review Resubmission

 

   

The Resubmission process is typically initiated by staff members to request that the Applicant/Grantee resubmit some or all of the application/grant requirements.

 

Specific instructions related to resubmission will be displayed in the Instructions section of the Resubmission page.

 

To request a resubmission:

 

1.Access the Resubmission page by clicking on the Add button from the Internal Review page.
2.Review the Grant Information.
3.Review the Task Information. Click on the View button to view the related full submission PDF.
4.Check the boxes next to the task requirements for which you are requesting a resubmission. Use the Check All box to check all requirements. Use the Clear All box to clear all the requirements, (The Task requirements will vary based on the task that is being reviewed.)
5.Review the contact information in the Review and Invite section of the page.
6.Click Save to save your changes. Click Save and Continue to save your changes and open the Resubmission Email page. Click Close to navigate back to the Internal Review page without saving any changes.
