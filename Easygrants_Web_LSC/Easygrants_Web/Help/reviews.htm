Reviews

 

   

The Reviews section of the Easygrants Manager allows you to manage the review cycles and stages and all aspects of review task items that are handled by reviewers typically external to the organization. These tasks include adding and editing reviewer contact information, communicating with reviewers, assigning reviewers to committees, managing reviewer conflicts and requests, assigning applicants to reviewers, managing the review process, and assigning final outcomes.

 

The Review Module consists of the following functionality:

 

•Reviews Easylist
•Reviewers Easylist
•Committees
•Review Processing

 

To access the Reviews section main page, click on the Reviews link in the left navigation. The Reviews Easylist page is displayed.
