Grant Contacts

 

   

The Grant Contacts tab allows users to manage and view person and organization contacts related to the grant project.

 

The grant contacts are displayed in the following sections:

 

•Summary
•Applicant/Grantee Primary Contact
•Applicant/Grantee Primary Contact History
•Applicant/Grantee Contacts
•Third Party Contacts
•Other Related Person Contacts
•Other Related Organization Contacts

 

To access the Grant Contacts page, use the Grants search functionality to locate a grant record. Select the Grant Info action and click on the Go button. The Grant Details page is displayed. Click on the Contacts tab in the grant record.
