Review Processing

 

   

The Review Processing section of the Easygrants Manager allows you to manage the committee (external) review. The external review consists of review cycles and stages.

 

Review Cycles are used to determine the participants (applicants or grantees) and reviewers (committee members) that will be involved in the review process. In addition, a custom preliminary automated conflicts and preferences process can be run to identify known conflicts, e.g., the applicant and the reviewer are at the same organization.

 

Review Stages are used to assign the required review tasks to the reviewers (committee members) and determine the review stage outcome (e.g., if the participant is eligible to move on to the next review stage) and, if the final review stage, the funding outcomes.

 

 

To access the Review Cycles section, click on the Reviews link in the left navigation. The Reviews Easylist page is displayed. Click on the Review Processing link in the left navigation. The Review Cycles page is displayed.
