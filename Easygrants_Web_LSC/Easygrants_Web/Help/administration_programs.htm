System Management

 

   

Within the Configuration section of the Easygrants Manager, administrative users can set up new or modify existing Programs, Funding Opportunities, and Funding Cycles with their associated workflow.

 

•A Program is the highest level of the funding hierarchy and is used to group Funding Opportunities.
•A Funding Opportunity is a grant-giving event. Funding opportunities may be a one-time opportunity or may occur repeatedly over time.
•A Funding Cycle is an instance of a Funding Opportunity. Funding Cycles may be open for a particular time frame, e.g., accepting applications or proposals for the first three months of every year. If the Funding Opportunity is a rolling opportunity, e.g., there is no deadline, there will only be one Funding Cycle.
•A Workflow is the path that the participants, public users and staff, will take to finish the grant lifecycle. This includes Eligibility, Application, Review, Grantee Reporting, and Validation rules.
