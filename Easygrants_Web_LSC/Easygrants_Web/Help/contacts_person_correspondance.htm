Person Correspondence

 

   

The person correspondence page displays all e-mail correspondence sent and logged from Easygrants related to the person contact record. This includes e-mails from workflow task submissions, one on one communication from the Action drop-down in Easylist pages, and communication made from the send e-mail functionality in the Basic Query Tool.

 

To access the Correspondence page, use the contacts search functionality to locate a person contact record. Select the action Contact Info and click on the Go button. The  Person Contact Details page is displayed. Click on the Correspondence tab. The Correspondence page is displayed.

 

The logged e-mails can be sorted by the column headers. Clicking once will sort the list in ascending alpha or numeric order. Clicking on the column header a second time will sort the list in descending alpha or numeric order.

 

Search results are initially displayed in sets of 10 or less records. At the bottom of the page, the total number of records will be listed (e.g., Viewing Records 1 - 10 of 30). To access the next set of records, if applicable, click on the Next 10 link. To access the last set of records, if applicable, click on the Last 10 link. To view all records, if applicable, click on the Show All link.

 

If the person contact has not received any e-mails sent from Easygrants, the section will display "This person contact has not been sent any logged e-mails from Easygrants.".
