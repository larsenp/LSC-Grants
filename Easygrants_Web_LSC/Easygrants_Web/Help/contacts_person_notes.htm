Person Notes

 

   

Contact Notes allows staff users to track additional information related to the person such as phone conversation information, internal meeting correspondence, or e-mail communication sent outside of Easygrants.

 

 

To access the Notes page, use the contacts search functionality to locate a person contact record. Select the action Contact Info and click on the Go button. The  Person Contact Details page is displayed. Click on the Notes tab. The Contact Notes page is displayed.
