Copying a Review Cycle

 

   

To copy of an existing review cycle:

 

1.Click on the Copy button next to the desired Review Cycle.
2.Click on the OK button in the pop-up confirmation to continue with copying the Review Cycle. The page refreshes and displays a new Review Cycle record listed as "Copy of [name of original review cycle]".

 

The Program, Time Period/Award Cycle, Review Cycle Name, and Description will be copied for the new review cycle. The selected Funding Opportunities and management items, participant and committees and requests and conflicts, will NOT be copied.
