Viewing Submitted Documents

   

The Submitted Documents section of the Grant Documents page displays all documents that were uploaded as part of a task submission, e.g., the Proposal Word document provided by the applicant during the Submit Proposal task.

 

To view the submitted documents, click on the View button next to the desired document in the Submitted Documents section of the Grant Documents page. The document is displayed in the original format (e.g., .doc, .xls, .rtf) in a new window.
