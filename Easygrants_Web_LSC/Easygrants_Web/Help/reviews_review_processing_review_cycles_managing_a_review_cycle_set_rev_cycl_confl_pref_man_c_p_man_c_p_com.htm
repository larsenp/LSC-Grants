Managing Conflicts and Preferences by Committee

 

   

Conflicts and preferences can be viewed, set, and updated by committee within the View and Modify Conflicts and Preferences section of the Conflicts and Preferences Processing page of the review cycle.

 

To view a conflict or preference for a committee:

 

1.Select the Committee name next to the View and Modify by Committee field.
2.Click on the Go button. The Conflicts and Preferences by Committee page is displayed.

 

The Conflicts and Preferences by Committee page displays the committee name and the current conflicts and preferences in the Conflicts and Preferences Details by Committee section with the following columns:

 

•Reviewer
•Reviewer Organization
•Committee
•Conflicts/Preferences

 

The results can be sorted by the column headers. Clicking once will sort the list in ascending alpha or numeric order. Clicking on the column header a second time will sort the list in descending alpha or numeric order.

 

In the Conflicts and Preferences Details by Committee section, the specific conflicts and preferences for the reviewer can be accessed. To access and view the specific reviewer conflicts and preferences, click on the View button for the desired reviewer. Refer to the Managing Conflicts and Preferences by Reviewer section for more information for adding, modifying, or deleting conflicts and preferences by reviewer.

 

To return to the Conflicts and Preferences Processing page for the review cycle, click on the Close button.
