Managing Person Information (Summary)

 

   

The person contact name, gender, and contact restrictions can be managed from the Summary section on the Person Contact Details page. Updating the e-mail address information will update that information in the appropriate information section as well.

 

To edit the basic contact information for a person:

       

1.Click on the Edit button in the Summary section of the Person Contact Details page. The Person Editor page is displayed.
2.Select the Prefix.
3.Modify the First Name (required).
4.Modify the Middle Name.
5.Modify the Last Name (required).
6.Select the Suffix.
7.Modify the Informal Name.
8.Modify the E-mail Address (required).

 

   

E-mail addresses in Easygrants must be unique. If the e-mail address being entered already exists in the system, a validation message will be displayed. Click on the Close button and return to the Contacts Easylist to search by the e-mail address.

 

9.Select the Gender.
10.Select if the contact should not be contacted.
11.Select if the contact is deceased.
12.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Person Editor page.
13.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Person Contact Details page.
14.To return to the Person Contact Details page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

The person records can be deleted from the Person Contact Details record. The Delete button is ONLY displayed if the person is not associated with any grant records or active task assignments (task assignments with a status of Incomplete or Complete).

 

To delete the person from Easygrants:

 

1.Click on the Delete button in the Summary section of the Person Contact Details page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.

 

   

Deleting the person is a hard delete. A history of the deletion action will exist only in the Database Log table.
