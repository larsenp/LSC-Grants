Replacing the Primary Contact/PI

 

   

The Primary Contact section of the Grant Contacts page displays the current primary contact associated with the grant and allows staff to replace the primary contact with another person contact in Easygrants or to easily forward the login information to the current primary contact.

 

To replace primary contact for the Grants project:

 

1.Click on the Replace button in the Primary Contact section of the Grant Contacts page. The Replace Primary Contact page is displayed.
2.Click on the Select button to select the new contact.
a)In Step 1 of the Person Selection pop-up window, enter part of the person’s last name and click on the Find button.
b)In Step 2, select the person’s name from the drop-down list and click on the Select button. The page will refresh and display the selected person’s name.

 

If the person contact is not found, click on the Close button in the Person Selector. In the Replace Primary Contact page, click on the Add New Person link and enter the contact information.

 

3.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Replace Primary Contact page.
4.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Contacts page.
5.To return to the Grant Contacts page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

The history of the modifications to the primary contact display in the Primary Contact History section of the Grant Contacts page.

 

To send the login information, click on the Send Password button. An e-mail will be sent to the primary contact's primary e-mail address from their contact record with the login information. The date last sent will be updated to the current date.
