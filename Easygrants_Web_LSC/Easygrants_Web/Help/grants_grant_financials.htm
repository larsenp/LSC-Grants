Grant Financials

   

The Grant Financials tab allows users to view and manage the budgets related to the grant (e.g., application, awarded, expenditures), the current payment schedule, the current reporting schedule, and the modifications made to the payment and reporting schedules by staff.

 

The grant financials are displayed in the following sections:

 

•Summary
•Budget
•Grant Fund Allocations
•Fund Allocation Summary
•Payment Schedule
•Reporting Schedule
•Grant Fund Allocations History
•Payment and Reporting Audit Trail

 

To access the Grant Financials page, use the Grants search functionality to locate a grant record. Select the Grant Info action and click on the Go button. The Grant Details page is displayed. Click on the Financials tab in the grant record.
