Managing Other Grant Contacts

 

   

The Other Grant Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page allows staff to manage person contacts that are not the primary contact or a related third party. Person contacts are affiliated to the grant with a particular type, e.g., financial, contractual, etc. The types are managed by an Easygrants administrative user in the Lookup Table Manager.

 

To add a grant person contact:

 

1.Click on the Add button in the Other Grant Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page. The Grant Person Contact Editor page is displayed.
2.Select the Grant Contact Type (required).
3.Select the person by clicking on the Select button (required).
a)In Step 1 of the Person Selector enter part of the person's last name and click on the Find button.
b)In Step 2 select the person from the drop-down list and click on the Select button. The page will refresh and display the selected person's name.

 

If the person contact is not found, click on the Close button in the Person Selector. In the Grant Person Contact Editor page, click on the Add New Person link and enter the contact information.

 

4.Enter the Description.
5.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Person Contact Editor page.
6.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Contacts page.
7.To return to the Grant Contacts page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit a grant person contact, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Other Grant Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page. The Other Person Contact Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the affiliation.

 

To delete a grant person contact:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Other Grant Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
