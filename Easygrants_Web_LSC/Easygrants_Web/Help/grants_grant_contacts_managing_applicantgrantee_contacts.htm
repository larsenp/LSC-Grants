Managing Applicant/Grantee Contacts

 

   

The Applicant/Grantee Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page lists all of the applicant/grantee contacts that were associated with the grantee project during the application phase of the grant lifecycle. The applicant/grantee contacts are entered by the primary contact/PI during the pre-application (e.g., Submit Letter of Intent) or application (e.g., Submit Proposal) tasks.

 

The Applicant/Grantee Contacts section displays with the following columns:

 

•Role
•Person Name
•Organization Name
•Grantee Type
•Primary

 

The records can be sorted by the column headers (except Primary). Clicking once will sort the list in ascending alpha or numeric order. Clicking on the column header a second time will sort the list in descending alpha or numeric order.

 

The records are initially displayed in sets of 10 or less records. At the bottom of the page, the total number of records will be listed (e.g., Viewing Records 1 - 10 of 30). To access the next set of records, if applicable, click on the Next 10 link. To access the last set of records, if applicable, click on the Last 10 link. To view all records, if applicable, click on the Show All link.

 

To add an applicant/grantee contact:

 

1.Select Person or Organization as the Grantee Type in the Applicant/Grantee Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page.
2.Click on the Add button. The Grant Primary Contact Editor page is displayed.
3.Select the Role (required).
4.If adding a Person Grantee Type, select the Contact Name by clicking on the Select button (required).
a)In Step 1 of the Person Selector enter part of the person's last name and click on the Find button.
b)In Step 2 select the person from the drop-down list and click on the Select button. The page will refresh and display the selected person's name.

 

If the person contact is not found, click on the Close button in the Person Selector. In the Grant Primary Contact Editor page, click on the Add New Person link and enter the contact information.

 

5.If adding an Organization Grantee Type, select the Organization Name by clicking on the Select button (required).
c)In Step 1 of the Organization Selector enter part of the organization's legal name and click on the Find button.
d)In Step 2 select the organization from the drop-down list and click on the Select button. The page will refresh and display the selected organization's legal name.

 

If the organization contact is not found, click on the Close button in the Organization Selector. In the Grant Primary Contact Editor page, click on the Add New Organization link and enter the organization information.

 

6.After selection of the person or organization, click on the Find Persons or Find Organizations button. The drop-down list will refresh to include person or organization contacts affiliated with the selected value.
7.Select the associated person or organization contact from the drop-down list.
8.Enter the Description.
9.Click on the Primary checkbox if this is the primary contact.
10.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Primary Contact Editor page.
11.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Contacts page.
12.To return to the Grant Contacts page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit an applicant/grantee contact, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Applicant/Grantee Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page. The Grant Primary Contact Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the applicant/grantee contact.

 

To delete an applicant/grantee contact:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Applicant/Grantee Contacts section of the Grant Contacts page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
