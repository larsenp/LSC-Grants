Review Cycles

 

   

Review Cycles are added in the Review Processing section of the Easygrants Manager. The review cycles features allows the user to:

 

•Add the Review Cycle.
•Identify the pool of participants (tasks to be reviewed, e.g, Submit Proposal or Submit Final Report) and reviewers (committee members). The pool of participants can be from multiple Funding Opportunities.
•Run a custom automated conflict check process (conflicts and preferences) to identify conflicts between the pool of participants and the reviewers on the selected committees.
•Modify the conflicts and preferences based on staff expertise.

 

Review cycles are comprised of one or more review stages. If the participant pool is going to be narrowed during the review process, there will be multiple review cycles.
