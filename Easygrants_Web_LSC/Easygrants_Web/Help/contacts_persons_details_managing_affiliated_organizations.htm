Managing Affiliated Organizations

 

   

Organization affiliations can be managed in the Affiliated Organizations section of the Person Contact Details page. Person contacts are affiliated to organizations with a particular role, e.g., staff member, board member, financial contact, etc. The roles are managed by an Easygrants administrative user in the Lookup Table Manager.

 

Persons can be affiliated to more than one organization. Only one of the affiliations can be the primary affiliation. Example: John Doe is the CEO of ABC Company and is also a Board Member of a University. John Doe would be affiliated to both organizations although his primary affiliation would be to ABC Company.

 

There can only be one primary contact for an organization.

 

To add an affiliation with an organization:

 

1.Click on the Add button in the Affiliated Organizations section of the Person Contact Details page. The Organization Affiliation Editor page is displayed.
2.Select the organization by clicking on the Select button (required).
a)In Step 1 of the Organization Selector enter part of the organization legal name and click on the Find button.
b)In Step 2 select the organization from the drop-down list and click on the Select button. The page will refresh and display the selected organization legal name.

 

If the organization contact is not found, click on the Close button in the Organization Selector. In the Organization Affiliation Editor page, click on the Add New  Organization link and enter the organization information.

 

3.Select the Type of Affiliation (required).
4.Enter the Job Titles.
5.Select if this is the primary organization for the person contact.
6.Select if this is the primary person contact for the organization.
7.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Organization Affiliation Editor page.
8.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Person Contact Details page.
9.To return to the Person Contact Details page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit an organization affiliation, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Affiliated Organizations section of the Person Contact Details page. The Organization Affiliation Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the affiliation.

 

To delete an organization affiliation:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Affiliated Organizations section of the Person Contact Details page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
