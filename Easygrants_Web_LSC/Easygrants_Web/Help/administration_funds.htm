Funds

 

   

The funds section allows the administrative user to designate the G/L accounts that will be used for grant payments. The total amount of the fund can be entered to help staff determine the amount available for award distribution. Once payments are entered and paid, the staff can then query to determine the remaining amount of the fund and modify the fund being used for future payments as needed.

 

The fund is associated with a funding cycle. All payments for grants associated to that funding cycle will default to using the fund selected. The fund can be modified on a case by case basis.

       

To access the Funds functionality, click on the Administration link in the left navigation. The Program Management page is displayed. Click Funds. The Grant Funds page is displayed.

 

The funds are initially displayed in sets of 10 or less records. At the bottom of the section, the total number of records will be listed (e.g., Viewing Records 1 - 10 of 30). To access the next set of records, if applicable, click on the Next 10 link. To access the last set of records, if applicable, click on the Last 10 link. To view all records, if applicable, click on the Show All link.

 

The funds can be sorted by the column headers. Clicking once will sort the list in ascending alpha or numeric order. Clicking on the column header a second time will sort the list in descending alpha or numeric order.
