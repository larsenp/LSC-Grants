Replacing the Primary Contact/PI

 

   

The Applicant/Grantee Primary Contact section of the Grant Contacts page displays the current primary contact associated with the grant and allows staff to easily forward the login information to the current primary contact.

 

To send the login information, click on the Send Password button. An e-mail will be sent to the primary contact's primary e-mail address from their contact record with the login information. The date last sent will be updated to the current date.

 

To replace the primary contact for the grant project, see the Managing Applicant/Grantee Contacts section.
