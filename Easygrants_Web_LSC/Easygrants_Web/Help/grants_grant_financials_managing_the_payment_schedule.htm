Managing the Payment Schedule

 

   

From the Payment Schedule section of the Grant Financials page, staff can add or modify the payment information for the grant. The payment schedule can be created either manually in the grant file or based on the designated schedule for the funding cycle, e.g., all grants for the 2008 funding cycle will have three payments over three years all being paid on the first of the year.

 

The payment information includes the scheduled information and the actual payment information that can be imported or manually entered based on the G/L Account information (e.g., actual payment date, check number). In addition, a contingency can be placed on the payment. The payment contingency is a reporting requirement that needs to be submitted by the grantee prior to the payment being approved.

 

For example, the second year's payment for the grant of $25,000 scheduled for 7/15 has a contingency that the Submit Progress Report due on 6/30 has an outcome of Approved by Staff. If the Submit Progress Report has not been submitted by 7/15, staff can see from the payment record the current outcome of the report (Not Submitted or blank) and not approve the payment.

 

To add a payment:

       

1.Click on the Add button in the Payment Schedule section of the Grant Financials page. The Payment Editor page is displayed.

 

The payment ID will be blank until the record has been saved.

 

2.Select the Payment Status (required).
3.Modify the Payee. The Payee field defaults to the legal organization name of the grantee organization.
4.Select the Payment Method (required).
5.Select the Fund Number. The fund will default to the funding cycle fund if one has been selected.
6.Enter the Requested Date (required).
7.Enter the Requested Amount (required).
8.Enter the Check/Payment Number (only if the payment has been made).
9.Enter the Payment Amount (only if the payment has been made).
10.Enter the Payment Date (only if the payment has been made).
11.Enter the Payment Notes.
12.Select the contingency Workflow Task. The list consists of all Grantee Reporting type tasks that have been added to the Reporting Schedule.
13.Select the Required Outcome.
14.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Payment Editor page.
15.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Financials page.
16.To return to the Grant Financials page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display a page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit a grant payment, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Payment Schedule section of the Grant Financials page. The Payment Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the payment.

 

To delete a payment:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Payment Schedule section of the Grant Financials page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
