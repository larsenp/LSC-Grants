Managing Grant Assurances

 

   

Details of the Grant Assurances are displayed in the Grant Assurances section, including the usage of the grants.

 

To edit grant assurance information:

       

1.Click on the Edit button in the Grant Assurances section of the Grant Details page. The Grant Assurances Editor page is displayed.
2.Edit the grant assurance information as applicable to the grant.
3.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Assurances Editor page.
4.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Details page.
5.To return to the Grant Details page without saving changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.
