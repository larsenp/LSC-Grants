Managing Grant Information

 

   

The Grant Information section of the Grant Details page displays the key information for the grant including the grantees (person and organization contacts), the project title and term, the funding, and the status.

 

To modify the grant information:

       

1.Click on the Edit button in the Grant Information section of the Grant Details page. The Grant Information Editor page is displayed.
2.Modify the Grantee Organization (required).
3.Modify the Primary Contact/PI (required).

 

To select the contacts, click on the Select button next to the field. The appropriate selector is displayed - Person or Organization Selector. Follow the steps to complete the selection process.

 

4.Modify the Project Title (required).
5.Modify the Project Description.
6.Modify the Grant Project Term (Start and End Dates).
7.Modify the Requested Amount.
8.Select the Status (required).
9.Select the Outcome.
10.Select the Subject Area.
11.Enter the Other Subject Area.
12.Select the Focus Areas.
13.Enter the Other Focus Area.
14.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Information Editor page.
15.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Details page.
16.To return to the Grant Details page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.
