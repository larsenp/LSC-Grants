Managing Grant Fund Allocations

 

   

From the Grant Fund Allocations section, staff can designate funding sources for the grant and set the amount of the fund allocated to the selected grant.

 

To add a fund allocation:

       

1.Click on the Add button in the Grant Fund Allocations section of the Grant Financials page. The Funding Source Editor page is displayed.
2.Select the Fund Name (required).
a)Enter at least two characters of the Fund Name; a drop-down will appear with funds that match.
b)Highlight the desired item and click Select. The page will refresh and display the Fund Number, Fund Description, Fund Type, Fund End Date, and Fund Balance for the selected funding source.
3.Enter the Grant Allocation (required).
4.Select the Allocation Status (required).
5.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Funding Source Editor page.
6.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Financials page.
7.To return to the Grant Financials page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display a page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

Payment Allocation is the sum of the Amounts from the Payment Schedule, irrespective of the payment status, for the funding source.  (Note: This calculation uses the Payment Amount if it exists, otherwise the Requested Amount will be used.)

 

Grant Allocation Balance is calculated based on the following formula: Grant Allocation Balance = Grant Allocation – Payment Allocation.

 

To edit a fund allocation, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Grant Fund Allocations section of the Grant Financials page. The Funding Source Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the record.

 

To delete a fund allocation:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Grant Fund Allocations section of the Grant Financials page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
