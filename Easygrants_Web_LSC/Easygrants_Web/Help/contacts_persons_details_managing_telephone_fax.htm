Managing Person Telephone/Fax Info

 

   

Multiple telephone and fax numbers can be tracked at the person contact level. The person telephone/fax numbers can include direct business lines, business mobile numbers, or personal mobile numbers. There can only be one primary telephone/fax numbers for a person contact.

 

To add a telephone/fax record:

 

1.Click on the Add button in the Telephone/Fax section of the Person Contact Details page. The Telephone/Fax Editor page is displayed.
2.Select if this is the primary telephone/fax number for the person.
3.Select the Type (required).
4.Enter the Phone Number (required).
5.Enter the Extension.
6.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Telephone/Fax Editor page.
7.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Person Contact Details page.
8.To return to the Person Contact Details page without saving changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit a telephone/fax record, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Telephone/Fax section of the Person Contact Details page. The  Telephone/Fax Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the number.

 

To delete a telephone/fax record:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Telephone/Fax section of the Person Contact Details page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
