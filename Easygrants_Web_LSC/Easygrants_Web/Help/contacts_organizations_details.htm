Organization Details

 

   

The Organization Details tab of the organization contact record provides key information including the ability to modify the organization information and person contacts affiliated to the organization.

 

The organization details are displayed in the following sections:

 

•Summary
•Organization Contacts
•Telephone/Fax
•Address
•Web Address
•Affiliated Organizations

 

To access the Organization Details page, use the contacts search functionality to locate an organization record. Select the action Organization Info and click on the Go button. The Organization Details page is displayed.

 

The Organization Details page can also be accessed by clicking on Organization Name links throughout Easygrants.
