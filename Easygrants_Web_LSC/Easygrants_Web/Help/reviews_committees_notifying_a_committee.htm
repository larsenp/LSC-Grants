Notifying a Committee

 

   

An e-mail notification can be sent to all members of a committee. Most commonly, this function is used to notify committee members that the review stage is open and they can begin to access their assigned tasks.

 

To notify members of a committee:

 

1.Click on the Send E-mail button next to the desired committee in the Committee Management section of the Committees page. The Send E-mail page is displayed.
2.Select a template or enter details of the e-mail message to be sent to the committee members.
3.Click on the Send button to send the message to all members of the selected committee. The E-mail confirmation page is displayed.
4.Click on the Close button to return to the Committees page.
