Introduction

 

 

The Easygrants Manager provides access to all levels of grants management system administration and process management through one easy-to-use online interface.

 

This Online Guide is intended to provide staff and administrative users of the Easygrants Manager tool with detailed instructions of system features and definitions of commonly-used Easygrants terms. For general assistance or technical support, contact Altum's support team.

 

The look and feel of some of the features within this guide may not always exactly replicate what you will see in Baseline or your client-specific implementation. We constantly strive to improve our Baseline product, and these improvements may be in advance of changes to this guide. Additionally, client implementations may represent client-specific requirements that are based on the Baseline product, but may not be included in the core Baseline feature set.
