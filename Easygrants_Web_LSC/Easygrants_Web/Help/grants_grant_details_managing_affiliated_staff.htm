Managing Grant Affiliated Staff

 

   

Staff and administrative users can be affiliated with a particular grant record for tracking the management of the grant project. Staff roles can include grant administrators, grants management, program officers, or program assistants. One staff person can be designated as the primary contact for the grant. A primary staff person can be assigned tasks directly in the workflow.

 

To add affiliated staff:

 

1.Click on the Add button in the Affiliated Staff section of the Grant Details page. The Grant Staff Affiliation Editor page is displayed.
2.Select the Primary checkbox if the affiliated staff is the primary contact.
3.Clicking the Select button for the Staff Person opens the Person Selector. The Selector is filtered to only allow the user to select Person Contacts that are Staff or Administrative users.
4.Select the type of affiliation the contact has with the grant.
5.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Staff Affiliation Editor page.
6.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Details page.
7.To return to the Grant Details page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit affiliated staff, click on the Edit button next to the desired record in the Affiliated Staff section of the Grant Details page. The Grant Staff Affiliation Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the affiliated staff.

 

To delete affiliated staff:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Affiliated Staff section of the Grant Details page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
