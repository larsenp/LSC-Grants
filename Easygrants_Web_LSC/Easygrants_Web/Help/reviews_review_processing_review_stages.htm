Review Stages

 

   

Once the Review Cycle details have been completed, the review stages for that cycle are created. Each review stage corresponds to a step in the review process, including reporting conflicts and preferences, submitting peer reviews (i.e., Initial Review, Preliminary Review, etc.), and conducting panel meetings.

 

Each review stage uses the participant and reviewer pool identified for the corresponding Review Cycle. Review cycle conflicts and preferences can also be included in the assignment process.

 

The review stage setup allows user to perform following:

 

1.Identify the review task (e.g., Report Conflicts and Preferences, Submit Preliminary Review Scores) to be executed for the stage.
2.Assign, view, and modify assignments of participants to reviewers.
3.Notify reviewers.
4.View and manage scores, comments, and stage and funding outcomes.

 

 

To access the Review Stages, click on the Reviews link and then the Review Processing link in the left navigation. Click on the Manage button next to the desired review cycle. The Participant/Committee Pool page is displayed. Click on the Review Stages tab for the review cycle. The Review Stages page is displayed.
