Grant Financial Summary

 

   

The Grant Financial Summary section displays summary information for awarded and payment amounts. The following fields are included in this section:

 

•Award Amount
•Payments Total
•Remaining Balance
•Remaining to Schedule

 

Remaining Balance is calculated based on the following formula: Remaining Balance = Award Amount - Payment Amount Total

 

Remaining to Schedule is calculated based on the following formula: Remaining to Schedule = Award Amount - Scheduled Payment Amount Total
