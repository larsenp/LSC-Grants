Managing a Review Stage

 

   

After a review stage has been added, the next step is to configure the details of the review stage, including assigning participant tasks (applications, proposals, grantee reports, renewals) to reviewers, sending e-mails to reviewers, managing the submission of review information, and determining outcomes for the review stage or for the funding cycle.

 

To access the management functions for a review stage:

 

1.Click on the Manage button next to the desired Review Stage. The Review Stage Assignments page is displayed.
