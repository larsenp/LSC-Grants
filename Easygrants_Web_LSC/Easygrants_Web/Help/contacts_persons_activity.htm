Person Contact Activity

 

   

The Activity tab in the person contact record displays related grant activity including grant records for which the person contact is the primary contact/PI and task assignments broken out by the task type.

 

The person activities are displayed in the following sections:

 

•Summary
•Grant Projects
•Application Tasks
•Review Tasks
•Grantee Reporting Tasks

 

 

To access the Person Contact Activity page, use the contacts search functionality to locate a person record. Select the action Contact Info and click on the Go button. The Person Contact Details page is displayed. Click on the Activity tab in the person record. The Person Contact Activity page is displayed.
