Contact Categories

 

   

The contact categories provides the functionality to group person contact records where there is no common data between the records.

 

Example: The granting organization sends out a monthly newsletter. The newsletter is sent to some of the grantees, the board of directors, the staff, and some press person contacts. Rather than pulling separate lists in the query tool, a contact category called "Newsletter" is created. All person contacts that should receive the newsletter are added to the group. The list can then be pulled altogether to send to the mail house from the Persons field group in the Basic Query Tool.

       

To access the Contact Categories functionality, click on the Contact Categories link in the left navigation. The Contact Categories page is displayed.

 

All contact categories are initially displayed.

 

The contact categories can be sorted by the column headers. Clicking once will sort the list in ascending alpha or numeric order. Clicking on the column header a second time will sort the list in descending alpha or numeric order.
