Managing a Review Cycle

 

   

After a review cycle has been added, the next step is to configure the details of the review cycle, including identifying the pool of participants (applicants or grantees), identifying the reviewer committee(s), completion of the conflicts and preferences process if applicable, and creating the appropriate review stages.

 

To access the Participants/Committees section, click on the Reviews link in the left navigation. The Reviews Easylist page is displayed. Click on the Review Processing link in the left navigation. The Review Cycles page is displayed. Click on the Manage button for a specific review cycle. The Participant/Committee Pool page is displayed.
