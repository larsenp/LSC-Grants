Managing Grant Award and Amendments

 

   

The Grant Award and Amendments section allows staff and administrative users to enter an initial award amount and amendments to the initial award. When adding, editing, or deleting an amendment to an initial award amount, the system will calculate the current Award Amount.  Every place the system displays the Award Amount will display the current award amount.

 

Example 1:

Initial Award Amount = $50,000.00

Award Amount = $50,000.00

 

Example 2:

Initial Award Amount = $50,000.00

Amendment Amount = $5,500.00

Award Amount = $55,500.00

 

 

To add an initial award:

       

1.Click on the Add button in the Grant Award and Amendments section of the Grant Details page. The Grant Award Editor page is displayed.
2.Enter the Award Date (required) and Award Amount (required).
3.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Award Editor page.
4.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Details page.
5.To return to the Grant Details page without saving changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit the initial award, click on the Edit button next to the initial award record in the Grant Award and Amendments section of the Grant Details page. The Grant Award Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the initial award.

 

To add an amendment:

       

1.Click on the Add button in the Amendments sub-section of the Grant Award and Amendments section on the Grant Details page. The Amendments Editor page is displayed.

 

The Amendments sub-section will not display until you add an initial award. See above to add an initial award.

 

2.Enter the Effective Date (required), Amendment Amount (required) and a Comment (optional).
3.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Amendments Editor page.
4.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Details page.
5.To return to the Grant Details page without saving changes, click on the Close button.

 

Saving the page without entering required information or with incorrect formatting will display page level validation in red text at the top of the page. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk.

 

To edit an amendment, click on the Edit button next to the amendment record in the Amendments sub-section of the Grant Award and Amendments section of the Grant Details page. The Amendments Editor page is displayed with the selected record information. Use the steps above to complete the modification of the amendment information.

 

To delete an amendment:

 

1.Click on the Delete button to the right of the desired record in the Amendments sub-section of the Grant Award and Amendments section of the Grant Contacts page.
2.Click on the OK button in the confirmation pop-up to continue with the deletion. The page refreshes and no longer displays the deleted record.
