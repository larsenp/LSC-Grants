Grant Demographics

   

The Grant Demographics tab allows staff to view and manage the project participant demographic information. The demographics can initially be entered by the applicant in the workflow task assignment (e.g., Submit Proposal).

 

For example, the grant project work will be related to women in rural areas. The demographics of Women and Rural will be selected in the Grant Demographics Editor.

 

The grant demographics are displayed in the following sections:

 

•Summary
•Demographics

 

To access the Grant Demographics page, use the Grants search functionality to locate a grant record. Select the Grant Info action and click on the Go button. The Grant Details page is displayed. Click on the Demographics tab in the grant record.

 

To edit the demographic information:

 

1.Click on Edit button in the Grant Demographics section of the Grant Demographics page. The Grant Demographics Editor page is displayed.
2.Select the participant demographics for Gender, Race/Ethnicity, Income, and Locale.
3.Click on the Save button to save the information and remain on the Grant Demographics Editor page.
4.Click on the Save and Close button to save the information and return to the Grant Demographics page.
5.To return to the Grant Demographics page without saving the changes, click on the Close button.
