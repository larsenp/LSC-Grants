Reviewer Information

 

   

The Reviewer Info tab in the person contact record allows staff users to mark person contacts as a reviewer and input key reviewer information, relate the person contact to review committees, and view the assigned reviewer tasks.

 

Only person contacts marked as a reviewer will be available in the Reviewers Easylist and available for committee membership.

 

The reviewer information is displayed in the following sections:

 

•Summary
•Reviewer Info
•Assigned Review Committees
•Review Tasks

 

 

To access the Reviewer Information page, use the contacts search functionality to locate a person contact record. Select the action Contact Info and click on the Go button. The Person Contact Details page is displayed. Click on the Reviewer Info tab. The Reviewer Information page is displayed.
