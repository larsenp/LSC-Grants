Subcommittees

 

   

The Subcommittees functionality supports the grouping of one or more reviewers from an existing committee allowing for a more detailed breakdown of the committee. Subcommittees are not used in review cycles or states.

 

To access the Subcommittees section, click on the Reviews link in the left navigation. The Reviews Easylist page is displayed. Click on the Committees link in the  left navigation. The Committees page is displayed with the Committee Management and Subcommittee Management sections.

 

The subcommittees are initially displayed in sets of 10 or less records. At the bottom of the section, the total number of records will be listed (e.g., Viewing Records 1 - 10 of 30). To access the next set of records, if applicable, click on the Next 10 link. To access the last set of records, if applicable, click on the Last 10 link. To view all records, if applicable, click on the Show All link.

 

The subcommittees can be sorted by the column headers. Clicking once will sort the list in ascending alpha or numeric order. Clicking on the column header a second time will sort the list in descending alpha or numeric order.
